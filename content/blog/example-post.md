## Why Complexity Matters for Cities

[placeholder] Cities are canonical examples of complex adaptive systems. Small changes in transportation policy can cascade through land-use patterns, housing markets, and social networks in ways that linear models fail to capture.

Understanding these dynamics requires tools that embrace non-linearity rather than averaging it away.
